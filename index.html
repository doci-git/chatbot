Lugagge Storage


Dear guest, Storing your luggage on-site after the check-out time, is challenging for us because we dont have a proper place were to store them, and we need to prepare rooms for upcoming guests.

Fortunately, there is a convenient luggage storage facility at the nearby train station, where you can securely leave your belongings. The cost is 1 euro per hour or 6 euros for the entire day. We appreciate your understanding and hope this alternative meets your needs.

Check the link below

https://leftluggageflorence.com/
